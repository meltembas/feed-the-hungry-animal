## ğŸšœ About The Project
This is a top-down arcade survival game where your only weapon is a pepperoni pizza.

You are a farmer standing your ground against a stampede of hungry animals. They come from the forest at random intervals and different speeds. Your goal? Feed them all. If even a single hungry animal gets past you, it's **Game Over**.

**Core Mechanics:**
* **Random Chaos:** Animals spawn at unpredictable times. You can never get comfortable.
* **Variable Speeds:** Some animals stroll, others sprint. You need quick reflexes to prioritize targets.
* **Projectile Physics:** Time your pizza throws perfectly to intercept the moving targets.
* **One Life:** No health bars. One mistake and the farm is lost.
